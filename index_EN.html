Why Free Coding Tour?

As a programming and artificial intelligence teacher for students with no prior knowledge and also university students, I always face two problems:

  1. different learning paces of each student
  2. constantly new content
This site tries to solve these problems in the following way:
  1. practical guide of problems with solutions to advance independently
  2. updated, free, and Spanish content for all students, current and past

From this, a new goal arises: to bring this educational and free material to Spanish speakers, since most free programming courses are in English, and the advancement of computing and artificial intelligence means that the most vulnerable Spanish-speaking sectors are falling behind if they cannot learn to use these new tools. FreeCodingTour.com is a small contribution to help people from different backgrounds and fields who want to learn to use them.

These courses are not a replacement for other courses, much less for the in-person support of teachers. You can find out here where I am currently teaching in-person classes.
